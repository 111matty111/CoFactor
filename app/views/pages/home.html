Mission

Empirical is a nonprofit, open source community building free education.
Over forty educators, developers, writers, and artists have contributed to Quill.



Impact

Quill provides interactive English grammar lessons
for middle school and high school students.

There are currently 10,000 students using Quill.



Interactive Learning

Quill presents students with problems to solve, and enables them to construct solutions.
Can you find and correct all nine errors in this passage?



Teacher Voice

Teachers play an active role in Empirical.
Their insights guide our product decisions.



Collaborative Development

Empirical is composed of independent teams.
Through Cofactor, Empiricists coordinate on projects.
